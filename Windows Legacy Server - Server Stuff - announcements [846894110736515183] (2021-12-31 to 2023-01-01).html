docR 14/07/2022 15:55
Hello all. I'm happy to see this community still thriving over a year since parting ways with its original owner. Over the course of the year, we've accumulated a wealth of information seldom found elsewhere to help people continue using their software of choice. I'm also happy to see how welcoming this group is. This sort of community element is not something I've come across in other similar-themed groups. Since this server's conception, we've always tried to strike a healthy balance with respect to just how involved we mods should be. For the most part, we've taken a night watchman sort of approach as most of you can conduct yourselves and resolve your disputes well. Unfortunately, as we've grown, so have our troubles with unruly members. While we appreciate a good sense of humor and healthy tomfoolery at times, we must maintain that this group is first and foremost an information archive. Newcomers should not feel overwhelmed when first joining, and the bulk memes shared here should be kept in their respective channels #deleted-channel . That isn't to say memes and jokes can't be shared elsewhere, we just ask that you try and keep this in moderation. We're going to be taking a slightly more active role in moderation from here onward. Don't be alarmed, this will still be the community you've come to love. Moreover, if you ever have a problem with a moderator or feel you've been wronged, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We only ask you watch your tongue and speak to us like a rational person would. Don't come in blazing using profanity at us or otherwise try to spark a public uprising over it. Thanks for being a part of our community!
